About

Peggy Yu

Pu-Hsuan Yu(余圃瑄) is a senior student majoring in Computer Science in the University of Taipei. During her college days, she has served as a representative of class and an officer of the committee of graduate students. In her free time, she enjoys swimming, playing volleyball and listening to music. She is an open-minded person who has a strong sense of responsibility, and she is willing to take up new challenges.

Activity

The activities that Pu-Hsuan planned in college life.

  • September 2014

    Freshman Welcome Party

    As the organizer and host of the Northern region freshman welcome party, she was in charge of planning and preparing for the event. Through this welcome party, freshman acclimated to the campus and obtained vital information about the Computer Science department.

  • October 2014

    Camping Activity

    She was a member of the life group.The core mission of the group was to take care of the safety of participants, make sure meal, accommodation, transportation has been well prepared and distributed. In the meantime, she portrayed the umpire in several games and performed dances on the campfire party. The event was held to enable freshman to get acquainted with their peer.

  • December 2014

    Christmas Party

    Christmas is a joyful holiday.The Computer Science department gathered at the Christmas party to celebrate this meaningful day. Students had a great time at the party, they enjoyed the hot pot, played games and exchanged gifts.

  • June 2016

    Farewell Party

    She was the vice coordinator of the farewell party. Her task was to manage the time schedule and allocate the jobs while junior students performed plays and danced for entertainment. They spared no effort to give the senior students a memorable send-off.

  • September 2016

    Graduation Student Association

    She is the group leader of the team shot, responsible for making contact to the manufacturers, and arranged the shooting schedule of all departments. As the same time, she needs to convey the graduation-related activities to all the classmates.
